  Item 8.01  Other Events.  
                            
On June 1, 2023, AppLovin Corporation (the “Company”) filed a prospectus    
supplement, dated June 1, 2023, to the Company’s shelf registration         
statement on Form S-3(File No. 333-272328)(the “Prospectus Supplement”). The
Prospectus Supplement covered 15,000,000 shares of the Company’s Class A    
common stock, par value $0.00003 per share to be sold by KKR Denali Holdings
L.P. (“KKR Denali”). The Company is filing this report to provide the legal 
opinion as to the validity of the securities covered by the Prospectus      
Supplement, which opinion is attached hereto as Exhibit 5.1 and is          
incorporated herein by reference. On June 1, 2023, the Company issued a     
press release announcing KKR Denali has entered into a purchase agreement   
pursuant to which it agreed to sell 15,000,000 shares of the Company’s Class
A common stock in a direct placement to client accounts managed by GQG      
Partners LLC, a global equity investment boutique. A copy of the press      
release is attached hereto as Exhibit 99.1 and incorporated herein by       
reference.
